Team Help-R

We are building a robot that will find people stuck in a salt mine (Help) and research it(R). This robot is remote controlled and transmits audio and video, exploring spaces that are hardly accessible to humans,colecting soil samples and measuring the psyhical and chemical parameters of the researched medium to establish if its fitting for life (pH of water, altitude difference, presence of toxic gas/of oxigen, radiation, vibrations) ,finds if there are beings found in difficulty, offers a minimum frst aid(medical kit) and transmits all the data to the command station, to be analized.
